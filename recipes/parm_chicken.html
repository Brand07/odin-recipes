Parm Crusted Chicken

The most delicious parmesean-crusted chicken you can make

Make juicy Parmesan-crusted chicken with this quick and easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cups Hellmann's Mayo
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 4 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 4 teaspoons Italian-seasoned dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Mix mayo and Parmesan together in a medium bowl until well combined.
  4. Arrange chicken on the prepared baking sheet. Spread mayonnaise mixture over top, then top with bread crumbs.
  5. Cook in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
