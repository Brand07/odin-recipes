Easy Alfredo Sauce

The easiest and tastiest alfredo sauce you can make!

This Alfredo sauce is the backbone of a complete, and completely easy, summer supper. The creamy, 5-ingredient sauce and your favorite long pasta noodle are all you need. Yes, you can add chicken or other protein â€” of course, we prefer summer vegetables, like vibrant tomatoes or squash, for a seasonal spin.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of garlic poweder
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 6 ounces of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Melt butter in a medium, non-stick saucepan over medium heat. Add cream cheese and garlic powder, stirring with a wire whisk until smooth. Add milk, a little at a time, whisking to smooth out lumps. Stir in Parmesan cheese and pepper.
  3. Remove from heat when sauce reaches desired consistency. Sauce will thicken rapidly. Thin with milk if cooked too long.
  4. Toss with hot pasta to serve.
